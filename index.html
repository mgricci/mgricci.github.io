Matthew Ricci
I am a postdoc at Brown University's Data Science Initiative advised by Stuart Geman. I study the optimal control of neural dynamics in large networks and am particularly interested in systems of coupled oscillators and neural synchrony. I maintain a secondary interest in the representation of syntactic structures in neural networks. I also work closely with the lab of my former dissertation adviser, Thomas Serre.
